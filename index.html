Hi, I am Ke Xu (徐科)

I am I am a Tenure-Track Associate Professor at School of Intelligence Science and Technology, Nanjing University. Preciously, I was a researcher at Huawei where I led the DataArts Insight team to build an intelligent, AI-augmented and easy-to-use Business Intelligene (BI) platform in the Huawei Cloud. I was a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the Visualization and Data Analytics Research Center (VIDA) at New York University (NYU), advised by Prof. Enrico Bertini and Prof. Claudio Silva.

I earned my Ph.D. in Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) in 2019, where I worked with my supervisor Prof. Huamin Qu and VisLAB.  Prior to my Ph.D. studies, I received my B.S. in Electronic Science and Engineering from Nanjing University (NJU) in 2015.

Contact:     xuke@nju.edu.cn |   Google Scholar | 

We welcome undergraduate/graduate students, post-docs, research assistants, and visiting interns/scholars. Please drop me an email with your research interests and CV if you are interested in our group.

 

Research

My research interests, broadly, are Data Visualization, Human-Computer Interaction, Big Data Analytics and Machine Learning. Specifically, I am interested in domains of visual anomaly detection, explainable AI, automated data insights, temporal data analytics and healthcare. My representative works include the ECGLens and Clouddet for visual anomaly detection in healthcare and cloud computing, respectively, the mTseer for explainable AI and a Survey for narrative visualization. I have won the ACM CHI 2018 Best Paper Hornorable Mention Award.
